Empowering Women in Tech Across Africa - Incubator Programme

The Empowering Women in Tech Across Africa incubator programme is a groundbreaking initiative designed to accelerate the growth and impact of women-led tech startups across the continent. This programme is dedicated to fostering innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurship among African women in technology by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and community support needed to thrive in the competitive tech industry.
